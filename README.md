# 📊 Mutual Fund Analytics Dashboard (BlueStock Capstone)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is a **FinTech Data Analytics Capstone** completed during my **Data Analyst Internship at BlueStock FinTech**.

The objective is to analyze mutual fund industry data from 2022–2025 using Python, SQL, Pandas, and data visualization libraries to uncover investment trends, investor behavior, fund performance, and portfolio insights.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze historical NAV performance of mutual funds
* Compare AUM growth across leading fund houses
* Study SIP inflow trends over multiple years
* Analyze investor demographics and geographic distribution
* Explore category-wise investment inflows
* Measure portfolio sector allocation
* Compute fund return correlations
* Generate business insights through Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## 📌 Key Insights

* Daily NAV trends reveal overall market growth with periods of volatility.
* SBI Mutual Fund consistently leads in Assets Under Management (AUM).
* SIP inflows reached record highs by the end of 2025.
* Equity funds dominate investor preferences.
* Most SIP investments originate from Tier-1 (T30) cities.
* Young and middle-aged investors contribute significantly to SIP participation.
* Banking and IT sectors form a substantial portion of equity fund portfolios.
* Strong positive correlations exist among several large-cap mutual funds.
